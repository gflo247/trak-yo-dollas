Why it's different

Built for people who care
where their data goes.

Most finance apps require handing over your bank credentials. We don't. You import a CSV — the same file your bank already gives you — and your device does all the work. Nothing leaves unless you choose to sync.

🔒

No bank connections

No Plaid, no OAuth, no screen scraping. You're always in control of what enters the app.

💻

Runs entirely in your browser

Your browser reads and stores everything locally. Refresh the page — everything's still there. Close the tab — nobody else has it.

📂

Your CSVs, your format

Works with exports from Chase, Ally, Fidelity, Vanguard, and most banks. Drop the file in — the app detects the format automatically.

🔁

Optional sync — never required

Sign in with Google to access your data across devices. Your data goes to Firebase (Google's cloud), secured by your Google account — not ours. Prefer to keep it local? Everything works without signing in.
