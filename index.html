What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

📊

Five ways to see your spending

Switch between a category treemap, income flow chart, daily heatmap, vendor breakdown, and month-over-month trend — all from the same data. Click any tile to filter instantly and find out that "miscellaneous" is mostly coffee shops.

💡

Try it before touching your data

Two fully-populated demo profiles let you explore every feature with realistic data. No CSV required to see what the app can do.

🏦

Full picture net worth

Checking, savings, investments, loans, real estate, and vehicles in one place. Save monthly snapshots and find out your net worth grew $18k last year — even when it didn't feel like it.

🧹

Smart auto-categorization, out of the box

A four-tier system categorizes transactions on import: your keyword rules first, then 280+ community-sourced patterns, then MCC codes from your bank, then 300+ built-in merchant keywords. Most transactions land correctly without any manual cleanup. Set additional rules for edge cases — they apply to every future import automatically.

🎯

Budgets and year in review

Set monthly limits per category and track pace in real time. Pull up an annual summary and find out you spent $4,200 on restaurants last year — then decide if that's fine or not.

🔄

Recurring charge detection

Surfaces subscriptions and regular charges from your transaction history automatically — including that $12.99 trial you forgot to cancel.

Privacy first

Built for people who care
where their data goes.

🔒

No bank connections

No bank logins, no screen scraping, no third-party data brokers. You import a CSV — the same file your bank or credit card already gives you — and nothing else enters the app.

💻

Runs entirely in your browser

Your browser reads and stores everything locally. Refresh the page — everything's still there. Close the tab — nobody else has it.

📂

Your CSVs, your format

Works with exports from Chase, Ally, Fidelity, Vanguard, and most banks. Drop the file in — the app detects the format automatically.

🔁

Optional sync — never required

Sign in with Google to access your data across devices. Your data goes to Firebase (Google's cloud), secured by your Google account — not ours. Prefer to keep it local? Everything works without signing in.

Getting started

Three steps.

1

Download your CSV

Usually under Statements or Download Activity in your bank or credit card portal.

2

Drop it in

Transactions categorize automatically using community rules, MCC codes, and 300+ built-in merchant keywords. Most land correctly on the first import.

3

Sync across devices (optional)

Sign in with Google to back up and access from any device. You don't need an account — your data saves locally either way.
