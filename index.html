Getting started

Three steps.

1

Download your CSV

Under Statements or Download Activity in your bank, credit union, or credit card portal.

2

Drop it in

Transactions categorize automatically. Most land correctly on the first import.

3

Sync across devices (optional)

Sign in with Google or a passwordless email link to access from any device. Everything works without an account.

What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

📊

Several ways to see your spending

📊 Bar charts 📈 Trend 📅 Daily heatmap 🏦 Flow ⊞ Split

Click any category tile to filter transactions instantly across all views.

📋

Your month in plain English

A short summary of what changed and why — lighter month, biggest mover, savings rate — written like a human, not a report.

⬇

Export your data anytime

Export transactions or budget history as a CSV, or export a full JSON backup — accounts, budgets, categories, and all transactions in one file. Import it back anytime to restore everything. Your data, portable, always.

📲

Works offline. Installs as an app.

Install it on any device — add to your home screen on Android or iPhone, or install from the address bar in Chrome on your computer. It runs in its own window like a regular app, no address bar in the way. Works without internet too; your data lives on your device.

🏦

Full picture net worth

Checking, savings, investments, loans, real estate, and vehicles in one place. Save monthly snapshots and watch your trajectory toward a goal.

Built for US, CA, UK, AU, NZ, and SG

The app supports banks and credit unions from all six countries by name. Auto-categorization covers local merchants — groceries, telecoms, transport, energy, food, and retail — in each market. Configure your currency symbol in settings: $ · C$ · £ · A$ · NZ$ · S$ · or any custom symbol.

🎯

Budgets that update with every import

Set monthly limits per category. Every import updates your pace — AT RISK warnings surface before you go over. 12-month history shows your patterns at a glance.

🔄

Recurring charge detection

Surfaces subscriptions and regular charges automatically — including that trial you forgot to cancel.
