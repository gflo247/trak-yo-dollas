💰 Income & savings rate

Your savings rate is how much of your income you keep each month. Choose how you want to track it:

Once saved, your savings rate will appear as a pill in the '26 Apr at a glance card on the Net Worth tab.

Manual — enter your take-home pay
Most accurate. Enter your monthly after-tax income once and we'll calculate your savings rate from your spending.
Auto-detect from deposits less accurate
Scans your imported transactions for deposits, excluding transfers and payments. Works best with bank (checking) CSV imports. May overcount if transfers appear as deposits.
