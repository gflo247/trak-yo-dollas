Not sure what to upload?

How to export from your bank:

  • Chase — Sign in → Accounts → Download activity → CSV
  • Amex — Sign in → Statements & Activity → Export → CSV (all activity)
  • Most banks: look for Download or Export in your transaction history

Columns the app reads:

  • Checking/savings — Date, Description, Amount, Type
  • Credit card (Chase, Amex, etc.) — Transaction Date, Description, Amount, Category
  • Debit/credit columns — Date, Description, Debit, Credit (or Withdrawals/Deposits)
  • Bank of America — Date, Description, Amount
  • Mint — Date, Description, Amount, Transaction Type, Category
  • YNAB — Date, Payee, Outflow, Inflow, Category
  • Monarch Money — Date, Merchant, Amount, Category

Format is detected automatically. If it's not right, use Having trouble? below.
