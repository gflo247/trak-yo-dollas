Your data & privacy

Simple, honest,
local-first.

No bank connections. No selling your data. Your CSV file(s) process in your browser. You control where they go from there - keep them local, or sync to Firebase via your Google account. Here's exactly what we do — and don't — with your information.

Last updated May 16, 2026

💾 Your data saves automatically

Everything — accounts, balances, imported transactions, budgets, and snapshots — saves automatically to your browser's local storage. It persists across refreshes and restarts on your device, even without signing in. Your browser processes CSV file(s) locally and nothing leaves your device unless you choose to sync. You can export your transactions as a CSV anytime from the Spending tab.

🔁 Sign in to sync across devices

Signing in backs up your full data — accounts, transactions, budgets, categories, and snapshots — directly to your Google account via Firebase. Readable only by you, enforced at the database level. Sign in on any device to restore everything instantly.

📊 Analytics — what's collected

Firebase Analytics automatically collects three event types: first_visit (first time you open the app), session_start (each visit), and page_view (each navigation). Each includes: browser language, page URL, screen resolution, and a randomly generated anonymous client ID. No names, emails, financial figures, or account data are ever included. All of this is blockable with any ad blocker.

🚫 What we never do

No bank connections. No screen scraping. No selling your data. No sharing your data with advertisers. No third-party trackers.
